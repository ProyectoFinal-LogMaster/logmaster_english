Who We Are?

We are LogMaster, a Uruguayan company currently working on a project for the professional technical-technological institution of Colón, focused on records of managment.

Our company, LogMaster, offers a records management application for educational centers.

We aim to become a source of support for educational institutions by offering new technology that transforms their record-keeping systems, significantly improving their efficiency and quality.
